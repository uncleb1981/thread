How it works

Tinder for your closet.

Browse women's closets in NWA who are your exact size. Heart the ones you love, skip the rest — it's that simple.

1

Set your sizes

Tell us your size in the brands you actually shop — we handle the cross-brand matching so you only see closets in your exact fit.

2

Browse & heart

Swipe through closet photos from women near you in NWA. Heart closets you like, skip the rest. When it's mutual, it's a match.

3

Meet & swap

Chat with your match to arrange a local meetup in Bentonville, Fayetteville, or Rogers. Exchange pieces you love — no money, no shipping.

Smart sizing

Finally — sizes that actually make sense.

A size 8 at Banana Republic is a 10 at Zara and a Medium at H&M. Thread NWA maps your sizes across brands so you only see women you'll actually fit.

📐

Cross-brand matching

We maintain a living database of sizing conversions across 50+ major retailers — from Anthropologie to Zara.

👗

Tops, bottoms, dresses & shoes

Set your size by category. Your top size doesn't have to match your bottom size — we handle each separately.

✏️

You're in control

Override any size with your real measurements. We'll use your actual body measurements to find the best fits.
