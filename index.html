Real sizing

One body.
Ten different sizes.
We match on all of them.

A size 8 at Madewell fits nothing like a size 8 at Zara. We collect your actual size in each brand you wear — so when we find you a match, the clothes will actually fit.

🏷️

Per-brand sizes, not guesswork

Tell us your size in the brands you wear. We match you with closets that carry the same brands in the same sizes — no surprises at the swap.

👖

Jeans by waist size

Denim sizing still varies wildly. We use your numeric waist size so your match's jeans will actually button.

👟

Shoes are straightforward

US women's shoe sizing is actually consistent. Size 8 is a size 8 — the one area brands mostly agree on.
