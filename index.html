Real sizing

One body.
Eight different labels.
We ignore them all.

Brand sizing is marketing, not math. A "Medium" at Old Navy is a completely different garment than a "Medium" at Free People. We skip the labels entirely and match on your actual measurements — the only number that doesn't lie.

📏

Measurements first, always

Enter your bust, waist, and hips in inches. That's it. We handle every brand translation behind the scenes.

👖

Jeans use your actual waist

We ask for your numeric waist measurement — not "Size 28" (which varies 1–2" across brands) but your real tape-measure inch.

👟

Shoes are straightforward

US women's shoe sizing is actually consistent. Size 8 is a size 8 — the one area brands mostly agree on.
